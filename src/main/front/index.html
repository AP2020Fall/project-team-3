Game Rules

The objective in Dots & Boxes is to capture more boxes on the board than your opponent.

Each player takes turn connecting a line between 2 adjacent dots either horizontally or vertically. Simple click between 2 available dots to create a line between them

Capturing a Box

When you place the 4th line that completes a box, rhat box will be captured by you, regardless whether any of the other lines were created by your opponent.

Endgame

The game end when there are no more dots to connect

The player that has captured the most boxes wins the game.
